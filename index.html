Frequently Asked Questions

Quik Cable provides a wide range of connectivity solutions, including high-speed internet, cellular plans, bundled packages, and streaming services. As a certified JNA dealer, we partner with top providers to offer the best options for your home or business.

As a certified JNA dealer, Quik Cable offers services from leading companies, including: Paramount+, SHOWTIME®, AT&T, Verizon, Spectrum, Comcast Xfinity, Frontier, HughesNet, CenturyLink, and many more. Through our partnerships, we ensure access to reliable and diverse service options.

Quik Cable is an authorized dealer and certified retailer, but not a direct service provider. We work with top providers, like those listed above, to connect you with their offerings, ensuring you receive the best possible service.

Quik Cable charges for execution services, including setting up new connections, processing bill payments, plan upgrades, account cancellations, and other administrative tasks. These charges range from $49.99 to $199.99, depending on the service request, and are separate from the billing charges of the service providers.

As a separate entity, Quik Cable independently handles the execution of your service requests. Our charges cover the work required to manage and complete requests such as plan upgrades, installations, or account changes. These fees are distinct from your provider's billing, which covers your actual internet, cellular, or streaming service.

You can reach our customer support team through the contact form on our website or via the live chat widget. Our team is available to help with inquiries, service requests, or troubleshooting needs.
